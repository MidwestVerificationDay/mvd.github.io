Midwest Verification Day 2024

MVD is an annual regional workshop on formal verification, and formal methods in general. Topics of interest include deductive verification, model checking, static analysis, abstract interpretation, runtime verification, model-based testing, and security. The workshop seeks to provide a forum for students and young researchers such as postdocs and junior faculty to present their work and ideas in an informal and relaxed setting, and share suggestions and ideas on the presented work. It is also meant to be an opportunity to network with fellow students, professors and people from industry.
